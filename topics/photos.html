Photos & Gallery

Learn how to take photos, view them in the gallery, and manage your photo collection.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Camera

Find and tap the Camera app icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Take a Photo

Point your camera at what you want to capture and tap the shutter button.

3

View Photos

Open the Gallery app to see all your photos. Swipe left or right to view different photos.

4

Manage Photos

Tap and hold a photo to select it. Then choose options like delete, share, or move to album.

Helpful Tips

  • Use volume buttons to take photos without touching the screen.
  • Tap to focus on specific areas before taking a photo.
  • Enable auto-backup to save your photos to the cloud automatically.

Common Issues and Solutions

Photos Not Saving

Check if your phone has enough storage space. Delete unnecessary files if needed.

Camera Not Working

Make sure you've granted camera permissions to the app in your phone settings.
