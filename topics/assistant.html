Google Assistant

Learn how to use Google Assistant to get help with tasks, answer questions, and control your phone.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Assistant

Say "Hey Google" or press and hold the home button to open Google Assistant.

2

Ask Questions

Ask any question or give a command, and the Assistant will respond or perform the task.

3

Get Responses

Listen to the response or read it on the screen. You can ask follow-up questions.

4

Follow Up

Continue the conversation by asking related questions without saying "Hey Google" again.

Helpful Tips

  • You can wake the Assistant from any screen by saying "Hey Google".
  • Use the Assistant to control smart home devices and set reminders.
  • Try asking for weather updates, news, or directions to places.

Common Issues and Solutions

Not Responding

Check your internet connection and make sure the Assistant is enabled in your phone settings.

Wrong Language

Go to Assistant settings and change the language to your preferred one.
