How to Text on WhatsApp

Learn how to send text messages on WhatsApp with this easy-to-follow guide.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open WhatsApp

Find and tap the WhatsApp icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Select a Contact

Tap on the contact you want to message from your chat list, or tap the new chat icon to start a new conversation.

3

Type Your Message

Type your message in the text box at the bottom of the screen.

4

Send the Message

Tap the send button (paper plane icon) to send your message.

Helpful Tips

  • You can use emojis by tapping the smiley face icon next to the text box.
  • To send a voice message, hold and hold the microphone icon while speaking.
  • You can attach files, photos, or documents by tapping the paperclip icon.

Common Issues and Solutions

Message Not Sending

Check your internet connection and make sure you have sufficient data.

Contact Not Found

Make sure the contact has WhatsApp installed and is using the correct phone number.
