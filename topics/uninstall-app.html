How to Uninstall an App

Learn how to remove apps from your phone with this easy-to-follow guide.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Settings

Find and tap the Settings app on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Go to Apps

Scroll down and tap on Apps or Applications in the Settings menu.

3

Select App

Find and tap on the app you want to uninstall from the list.

4

Uninstall App

Tap the Uninstall button and confirm when prompted.

Helpful Tips

  • Some system apps cannot be uninstalled as they are essential for your phone.
  • Uninstalling an app will remove all its data from your phone.
  • You can reinstall apps later from the app store if needed.

Common Issues and Solutions

Can't Find App

Use the search bar in Settings to find the app you want to uninstall.

Uninstall Button Grayed Out

This means the app is a system app and cannot be uninstalled.
