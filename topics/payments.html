Online Payments

Learn how to make secure online payments using UPI, mobile wallets, and other digital payment methods.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Choose Payment App

Install and open a payment app like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm from the Play Store.

2

Link Bank Account

Add your bank account details and verify your identity using your Aadhaar number or PAN.

3

Make Payment

Enter the recipient's UPI ID or scan their QR code, enter the amount, and confirm the payment.

4

Verify Transaction

Check the transaction status and save the payment receipt for future reference.

Helpful Tips

  • Always verify the recipient's details before making a payment.
  • Keep your UPI PIN and app passwords secure and never share them.
  • Enable transaction notifications to track your payments.

Common Issues and Solutions

Payment Failed

Check your internet connection and ensure you have sufficient balance in your account.

Wrong UPI ID

Double-check the UPI ID before confirming the payment to avoid sending money to the wrong person.
