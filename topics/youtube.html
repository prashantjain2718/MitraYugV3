How to Use YouTube

Learn how to watch videos, subscribe to channels, and manage your YouTube experience.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open YouTube

Find and tap the YouTube app icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Search for Videos

Tap the search bar at the top and type what you want to watch.

3

Watch Videos

Tap on a video to play it. Use the play/pause button to control playback.

4

Subscribe to Channels

Tap the Subscribe button on any channel to get notified about new videos.

Helpful Tips

  • Double-tap the left or right side of the video to rewind or fast forward.
  • Tap the three dots menu to find options like save to playlist or share.
  • Enable autoplay to keep watching related videos automatically.

Common Issues and Solutions

Video Not Playing

Check your internet connection and try closing and reopening the app.

Poor Video Quality

Tap the three dots menu, select Quality, and choose a higher resolution.
