Booking Rides

Learn how to book rides using popular ride-hailing apps like Uber and Ola.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open App

Open the Uber or Ola app on your phone and sign in to your account.

2

Set Location

Enter your pickup location and destination, or use the map to select locations.

3

Choose Ride

Select your preferred ride type (Auto, Mini, Prime, etc.) and see the estimated fare.

4

Book Ride

Tap "Book" or "Confirm" to request a ride. Wait for a driver to accept your request.

Helpful Tips

  • Enable location services for accurate pickup and drop-off points.
  • Check the driver's rating and vehicle details before accepting the ride.
  • Use the "Share Trip" feature to let family know your location.

Common Issues and Solutions

No Drivers Available

Try during non-peak hours or wait a few minutes and try again.

Wrong Location

Use the map to manually select your exact pickup or drop-off point.
