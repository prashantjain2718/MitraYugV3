WiFi & Hotspot

Learn how to connect to WiFi networks and use your phone as a mobile hotspot.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Settings

Find and tap the Settings app on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Connect to WiFi

Tap on WiFi, then select your network from the list and enter the password if required.

3

Enable Hotspot

Go to Hotspot settings and tap to enable it. Set a name and password for your hotspot.

4

Share Connection

Other devices can now connect to your hotspot using the name and password you set.

Helpful Tips

  • Save WiFi networks to connect automatically when in range.
  • Use a strong password for your hotspot to keep it secure.
  • Monitor data usage when using hotspot to avoid exceeding your plan.

Common Issues and Solutions

Cannot Connect

Check if the WiFi is turned on and you're within range of the network.

Slow Connection

Try moving closer to the router or check if too many devices are connected.
