Making a Phone Call

Learn how to make a phone call using your smartphone. This guide will help you call your friends and family easily.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open the Phone App

Look for the phone icon on your home screen. It usually looks like a green phone receiver. Tap on it to open the phone app.

2

Enter the Phone Number

You'll see a keypad with numbers. Enter the phone number you want to call. Make sure to include the area code if needed.

3

Start the Call

Look for the green phone button with a phone icon. Tap it to start the call. Wait for the other person to answer.

4

End the Call

When you're done talking, tap the red phone button to end the call. This button usually appears at the bottom of the screen.

Helpful Tips

  • Make sure your phone has enough battery before making a call
  • Check if you have good network signal (bars at the top of your screen)
  • You can save frequently called numbers in your contacts for easier access
  • If the call doesn't connect, try again after a few seconds

Common Issues and Solutions

No Network Signal

Move to an area with better network coverage or try again later.

Wrong Number

Double-check the number before calling. You can also save important numbers in your contacts.

Call Not Connecting

Try turning your phone's airplane mode on and off, or restart your phone.
