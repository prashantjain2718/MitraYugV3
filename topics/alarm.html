Setting Alarms

Learn how to set and manage alarms on your phone.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Clock App

Find and tap the Clock app icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Add New Alarm

Tap the + button or "Add Alarm" to create a new alarm.

3

Set Time

Use the time picker to set your desired alarm time.

4

Save Alarm

Tap "Save" or "Done" to set the alarm. You can enable/disable it using the switch.

Helpful Tips

  • Set multiple alarms for different times of the day.
  • Choose different sounds for different alarms.
  • Use the snooze feature to delay the alarm by a few minutes.

Common Issues and Solutions

Alarm Not Sounding

Check if your phone is in silent mode and the volume is turned up.

Wrong Time

Make sure your phone's time and timezone settings are correct.
