How to Use Email

Learn how to send and receive emails with this easy-to-follow guide.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Email App

Find and tap the Gmail or Email app icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Compose New Email

Tap the compose or new email button (usually a pencil or plus icon).

3

Fill Email Details

Enter the recipient's email address, subject, and your message in the appropriate fields.

4

Send Email

Tap the send button (paper plane icon) to send your email.

Helpful Tips

  • You can attach files by tapping the paperclip icon before sending.
  • To reply to an email, tap the reply button in the email thread.
  • You can organize emails into folders for better management.

Common Issues and Solutions

Email Not Sending

Check your internet connection and make sure you've entered the recipient's email address correctly.

Can't Find Emails

Use the search bar at the top of the app to find specific emails by sender or subject.
