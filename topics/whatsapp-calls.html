WhatsApp Calls & Video Calls

Learn how to make voice and video calls on WhatsApp with this easy-to-follow guide.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open WhatsApp

Find and tap the WhatsApp icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Select a Contact

Tap on the contact you want to call from your chat list.

3

Start the Call

For voice calls, tap the phone icon. For video calls, tap the video camera icon.

4

End the Call

Tap the red end call button to hang up when finished.

Helpful Tips

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection for clear calls.
  • You can switch between voice and video calls during the call.
  • Use headphones for better audio quality and privacy.

Common Issues and Solutions

Poor Call Quality

Check your internet connection and try moving to an area with better network coverage.

Camera Not Working

Make sure you've granted camera permissions to WhatsApp in your phone settings.
