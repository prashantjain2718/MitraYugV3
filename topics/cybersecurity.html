Cybersecurity

Learn how to protect your phone and personal information from online threats.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Set Screen Lock

Go to Settings > Security and set up a PIN, pattern, or biometric lock for your phone.

2

Install Security App

Download and install a trusted antivirus app from the Play Store to protect against malware.

3

Update Regularly

Keep your phone's software and apps up to date to get the latest security patches.

4

Secure Accounts

Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication for your important accounts.

Helpful Tips

  • Never share your passwords or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank.
  • Be cautious when clicking on links in messages or emails from unknown sources.
  • Regularly backup your important data to cloud storage or external devices.

Common Issues and Solutions

Suspicious Activity

If you notice unusual activity, change your passwords immediately and contact your bank.

Unknown Apps

Regularly check your installed apps and uninstall any that you don't remember installing.
