Keyboard Language

Learn how to change your keyboard language and type in different languages.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Settings

Find and tap the Settings app on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Go to Language Settings

Scroll down and tap on System, then Languages & input, and finally Virtual keyboard.

3

Add New Language

Tap on your keyboard app (like Gboard) and select Languages. Tap Add keyboard to add a new language.

4

Switch Languages

When typing, tap the globe icon on your keyboard to switch between languages.

Helpful Tips

  • You can have multiple languages installed at once and switch between them easily.
  • Use the microphone icon to speak and let the keyboard translate your voice to text.
  • Enable auto-translate to automatically translate text as you type.

Common Issues and Solutions

Wrong Language

Tap the globe icon on your keyboard to switch to the correct language.

Language Not Available

Go to keyboard settings and download the language pack for your desired language.
