Using Google Maps

Learn how to navigate, search for places, and get directions using Google Maps.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Google Maps

Find and tap the Google Maps app icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Search for Places

Tap the search bar at the top and type the name of a place you want to find.

3

Get Directions

Tap on a place, then tap "Directions" to get step-by-step navigation.

4

Start Navigation

Choose your mode of transport and tap "Start" to begin navigation.

Helpful Tips

  • Enable location services for accurate navigation.
  • Download offline maps for areas with poor internet connection.
  • Use voice navigation for hands-free guidance.

Common Issues and Solutions

Location Not Accurate

Make sure GPS is enabled and you're outdoors or near a window.

Navigation Not Working

Check your internet connection and try closing and reopening the app.
