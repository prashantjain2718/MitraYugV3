Google Search

Learn how to effectively search for information using Google Search.

Video tutorial will be available soon

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Open Google

Find and tap the Google app icon or open your web browser and go to google.com.

2

Type Your Search

Tap the search bar and type what you want to find. Be specific for better results.

3

View Results

Scroll through the search results and tap on the most relevant link.

4

Refine Search

Use filters like Images, News, or Videos to find specific types of content.

Helpful Tips

  • Use quotation marks to search for exact phrases.
  • Add site: before a website to search within that site.
  • Use the microphone icon for voice search.

Common Issues and Solutions

No Results Found

Try using different keywords or check your spelling.

Slow Loading

Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page.
