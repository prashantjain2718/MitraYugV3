About MitraYug

Empowering Senior Citizens with Digital Literacy

Our Mission

MitraYug is dedicated to making technology accessible to senior citizens by providing easy-to-understand digital literacy resources. We believe that everyone, regardless of age, should have the opportunity to benefit from modern technology.

Our Vision

We envision a world where senior citizens are confident and comfortable using digital devices and services, enabling them to stay connected with loved ones, access information, and participate in the digital economy.

Our Values

Empathy

We understand the unique challenges faced by senior citizens and design our resources accordingly.

Education

We believe in continuous learning and provide comprehensive, easy-to-follow tutorials.

Community

We foster a supportive learning environment where everyone can help each other grow.

Trust

We prioritize user safety and provide reliable, accurate information.
